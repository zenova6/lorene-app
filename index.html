What Lorene does

Everything your server needs.

Six packed modules — each one built for maximum chaos and community engagement.

🎯
Trivia & Fun Games
Challenge your server with trivia questions across dozens of topics. Keep score, build streaks, and crown the smartest person in the chat.
/trivia /quiz /riddle
🔥
Roasts & Quotes
Generate savage roasts or drop wisdom bombs with random quotes. Roast your friends, lift your spirits — Lorene's got range.
/roast /quote /compliment
🎣
Fishing System
Cast your rod, reel in fish of all rarities — from common sardines to legendary sea creatures. Build your collection and sell your catch for racks.
/fish /inventory /sell
💰
Economy & Racks
A full server economy built around racks — earn them, spend them, gamble them. Daily rewards, work commands, heists, and more. Build your empire or lose it all in the slots. The choice is yours.
/balance /daily /work /slots /heist /leaderboard
📈
Fake Stock Market
Buy and sell fake stocks inside your server. Prices fluctuate in real time. Play the market, short your enemies, become the Wolf of Discord.
/buy /sell stock /portfolio

More Fun Commands
